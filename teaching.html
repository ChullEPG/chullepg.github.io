Teaching

Chris has taught several courses at Stanford, in the Economics, Engineering, Management Science & Engineering departments. He has also taught several courses and intensive summer boot camps for high school and undergraduate students at Oxford. He has experience developing course syllabi, exams, lectures, and homework materials. He was part of the development of the online Canvas platform for the world renowned online tetbook CORE-Econ (link), world-renowned Economics textbook.
